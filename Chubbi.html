Chubbi

Introducing a project that's sure to whet your appetite: a food reviewing and rating app that takes a fresh approach to culinary criticism. Rather than reviewing entire restaurants, this app focuses on individual menu items.
As part of the development team, I worked on the UI of the app, ensuring that the user experience was intuitive, engaging, and visually appealing. From designing sleek and simple pages to crafting eye-catching icons, I was responsible for creating an interface that put the emphasis on the food, while still allowing users to easily navigate the app and share their own reviews and ratings.
If you're a foodie looking to discover new dishes, or just someone who wants to share your thoughts on your favorite meals, this app is the perfect platform. And with its user-friendly design and emphasis on the individual menu item, it's never been easier to share your culinary opinions with the world. So why wait? Download the app today and start rating and reviewing your favorite dishes!
